Senthilnatha Murugan

Santoshi Mata (Hindi: संतोषी माता) or Santoshi Maa (संतोषी माँ) is a relatively new goddess in the Hindu pantheon. She is venerated as "the Mother of Satisfaction",[1] the meaning of her name. Santoshi Mata is particularly worshipped by women of North India and Nepal. A vrata (ritual fast) called the Santoshi Maa vrata performed by women on 16 consecutive Fridays wins the goddess' favour.

To the North of Santhoshimatha Lors Senthilnatha Murugan is Placed. Call him Karthikeya; Call him Murugan; Call him Skanda, Subramanya, Shanmuga, Senthilnatha, Swaminatha, Saravanabahva,,or a thousands other names of devotion, he holds a very important place in the Hindu ethos. He is revered in many countries across the world as an important deity in the Hindu Pantheon.
